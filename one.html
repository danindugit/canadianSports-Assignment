Toronto Blue Jays

Team Description

The Toronto Blue Jays are a professional baseball team in Major League Baseball (MLB). They are the only Canadian team in the MLB. Hence, their slogan; One team, One nation. Representing all of Canada from Toronto, Ontario, the Blue Jays have had a very significant on the league, and on the sport of baseball as a whole.

Team History

The Toronto Blue Jays Franchise was founded in 1977, 108 years after the MLB was founded. Making 8 playoff apperances, winning back-to-back championships in 1992 and 1993, claiming 6 East Division titles and 2 American League penants, the Jays have built themselves an impressive resume.

All-time Greats:

  1. Roy Halladay
    • 2003 American League Cy Young Award
    • 2010 National League Cy Young Award
    • 2011 Best Major League Baseball Player ESPY Award
    • 2011 Best Moment ESPY Award
  2. Jose Bautista
    • Esurance's MLB Award for Best Play, Offense (2015)
    • 2× Hank Aaron Award (2010, 2011)
    • John Cerutti Award (2010)
    • 6× Major League Baseball All-Star (2010–15)
    • 2× Neil MacCarl Award (2010, 2011)
    • 3× Silver Slugger Award at outfield (2010, 2011, 2014)
    • Iconic Moment: "The bat flip"
  3. Joe Carter
    • 5× All-Star (1991, 1992, 1993, 1994, 1996)
    • 2× Silver Slugger Award winner (1991, 1992)
    • In 1988, Carter was inducted into the Wichita State University Pizza Hut Shocker Hall of Fame.
    • In 1999, Carter was inducted into the Missouri Valley Conference Hall of Fame.
    • In 2003, Carter was inducted into the Canadian Baseball Hall of Fame.
    • In 2004, Carter was inducted into the Ontario Sports Hall of Fame.
    • Iconic Moment: Walks off the 1993 World Series
  4. Josh Donaldson
    • American League Most Valuable Player Award (2015)
    • Hank Aaron Award (2015)
    • 2× Home Run Derby participant (2014, 2015)
    • Fielding Bible Award (2014)
    • 3× MLB All-Star (2014–16)
    • 2× Silver Slugger Award at third base (2015, 2016)

Current Team

Currently the Toronto Blue Jays are playing with Dunedin as their home location due to the COVID-19 pandemic. The team is young and fresh off a rebuild. Top prospects and talents include Vladimir Guerrero Jr., Bo Bichette, and Hyun-jin Ryu. The Jays look to make a strong playoff run this year, so this might be the perfect time to start being a Blue Jays Fan
